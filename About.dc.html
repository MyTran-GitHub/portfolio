About me

I'm curious, and I care.

The pattern

Across four very different fields, I kept running into the same structural problem.

Climate adaptation Agriculture Wildfire risk Urban environments

Environmental decisions are often made without reliable, comparable evidence of outcomes.

The focus

My work is shaped by this gap: building Earth observation systems that go beyond measuring environmental change to understanding the impact of interventions under real-world uncertainty
